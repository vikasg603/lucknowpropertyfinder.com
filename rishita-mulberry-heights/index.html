Welcome to Rishita Mulberry Heights

Rishita Mulberry Heights is a prominent township at Sushant Golf City in Lucknow. The multiple towers offer 2 and 3 BHK apartments with private balconies. The aesthetic interiors of the apartments are complemented with contemporary features. The well-designed apartments provide generous spaces, ventilation and unstinted views of the golf and surrounding greens. The gated community comprises 80% of open spaces and numerous amenities. Indulge yourself in amenities like a yoga centre, clubhouse, swimming pool, cricket pitch, jogging track, gymnasium, tennis court, multipurpose hall, kids play area, etc.

The booming neighbourhood of Sushant Golf City is equipped with the finest infrastructure facilities. GD Goenka International School, Seth M R Jaipuria School, Medanta Hospital, Wellsun Super Speciality Hospital, Ekana International Stadium, Phoenix Palassio and ISKCON Temple are in the vicinity. The Amar Shaheed Path, Outer Ring Road and Sultanpur Road provide broader connectivity. The international airport and Charbagh railway station are easily accessible from here.
